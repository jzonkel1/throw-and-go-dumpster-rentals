Common Questions

How much does a dumpster rental cost in Corpus Christi?
Our 15-yard is $400 and our 20-yard is $450. Both include delivery, pickup, a 5-day rental window, and a 2-ton (4,000 lb) weight allowance. No hidden fees — return it early and we credit $25 toward your next rental.
How fast can you deliver?
In most cases we offer same-day or next-day delivery across the Corpus Christi area. Call (361) 317-5001 and we'll get you on the schedule right away.
What can and can't go in the dumpster?
You can toss household junk, furniture, renovation and construction debris, roofing, and yard waste. We can't take hazardous materials, wet paint, chemicals, tires, or appliances containing refrigerant. If you're not sure about an item, just ask.
How long can I keep the dumpster?
Standard rentals run up to 5 days. Need it longer or need a swap-out mid-project? No problem — just let us know and we'll work around your timeline.
Will it fit in my driveway without damaging it?
Yes. Our roll-offs are designed for residential driveways and we place them carefully. Let us know about tight spots or delicate surfaces and we'll take extra care.
What size dumpster do I need?
For a single room, garage, or small roof, the 15-yard is usually plenty. For a whole-home cleanout, roofing tear-off, or bigger remodel, go with the 20-yard. Not sure? Call and describe the job — we'll help you choose.
What areas do you serve?
Corpus Christi and the surrounding Coastal Bend — including Calallen, Portland, Robstown, Ingleside, Aransas Pass, Flour Bluff, Annaville and Gregory. Not sure about your spot? Give us a call.
Do you offer contractor or volume pricing?
Yes. If you rent regularly or need multiple containers, we'll set you up with volume pricing and priority scheduling. Call (361) 317-5001.
