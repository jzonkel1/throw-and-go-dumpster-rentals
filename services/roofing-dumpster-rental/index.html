Good to Know

Roofing Tear-Off — FAQs

What size dumpster do I need for a roof tear-off?
For most single-layer residential roofs up to about 30 squares, a 15-yard works. For larger roofs, multiple layers, or steeper pitches, go with the 20-yard. We can walk you through it on the phone in two minutes.
Will roofing shingles go over the weight limit?
Both sizes include 2 tons (4,000 lbs), which covers most residential tear-offs. Very large or multi-layer roofs can exceed that; we'll tell you upfront if a swap-out or overage might apply so there are no surprises.
Can you deliver in time for my roofing crew?
Yes — we offer same and next-day delivery and can place the container the evening before so your crew starts on time. Roofers get priority scheduling.
