Good to Know

Construction & Reno — FAQs

What size dumpster is best for a kitchen or bathroom remodel?
A single-room remodel usually fits in a 15-yard. If you're doing a large kitchen, multiple rooms, or a whole-home renovation, go with the 20-yard or plan on a swap-out.
Can you do dumpster swap-outs during a long project?
Absolutely. When your container fills up, call us and we'll haul the full one and drop an empty so your crew never has to stop. Contractors get priority scheduling.
Do you offer contractor or volume pricing?
Yes. If you're running ongoing projects or need multiple containers, call (361) 317-5001 and we'll set you up with volume pricing.
