The Local Alternative to the 1-800 Guys

When you call Throw & Go, you're not routed through a national call center. You're talking to the same local crew that shows up with the truck. That's the whole point — Matt and his team run this business right here in Corpus Christi, and they treat every job like it's in their own neighborhood, because it usually is.

We started Throw & Go because dumpster rental in the Coastal Bend was harder than it needed to be: unclear pricing, surprise fees, and companies that didn't call you back. We do the opposite. Two straightforward sizes, flat pricing with 2 tons and 5 days included, and a phone that actually gets answered.
